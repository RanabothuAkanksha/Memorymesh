# MemoryMesh

## Overview

MemoryMesh is an AI-powered organizational memory platform.

Instead of searching documents, users can ask:

- Why was a decision made?
- Who proposed it?
- What alternatives were considered?
- What systems depend on it?

The platform reconstructs historical organizational reasoning from documents, discussions, and meetings.

---

## Problem

Organizations lose critical knowledge when:

- Employees leave
- Teams reorganize
- Documentation becomes outdated

Important context becomes fragmented across:

- Slack
- Notion
- Meetings
- Tickets
- Emails

MemoryMesh creates a living knowledge graph of organizational decisions.
